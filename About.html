About

    My name is Thanh Le. I was born and raised in Viet Nam until I was 17 years old. At the age of 17, I was sent to the United State of America to study abroad and I haved stayed in the US eversince. My artworks are heavily influenced by my culture and my experiences. Since I opened to new idea all the time, you may see artworks with uncoventional materials, artworkswith science humor, artworks that take you to the past of Asia.
